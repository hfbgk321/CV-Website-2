My journey began at Brooklyn Technical High School, where I attended an introduction to CS course. There my teachers, Mr. Folwell and Mr.Levin introduced me to the endless possibilities that coding brought. Throughout my academic career, I can say that I have truely fallen for the process and creativity brought about by coding. The ability to create something that can be so impactful to people without much except the passion and motivation to learn really resignated with me.

This passion of mine has peaked these past few months as the hunt for real-world experience in the tech industry fired up. Despite having already worked at multiple tech firms, I know that in order for me to be the best Software engineer that I can be, the learning must never stop. That being said, the hunt for experience willl also never stop.
